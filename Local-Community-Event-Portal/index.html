Community Bulletin

The Local Community Event Portal supports residents through workshops, awareness programs, environmental initiatives, health camps, sports activities, and cultural celebrations. Community members are encouraged to participate in volunteer activities, social development programs, and educational workshops that strengthen local engagement. The city council regularly organizes events to improve public spaces, increase environmental awareness, and promote healthy lifestyles for all citizens. Residents can register online, receive event notifications, and access updated schedules through the portal.
